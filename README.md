Aevoria is not just a space mining game; it is a high-fidelity governance simulator. You will command an SSTO (Single-Stage-To-Orbit) asteroid mining heavy-lift fleet, deploy ethical AI miner swarms, and build a zero-waste interplanetary civilization. But the vacuum of space isn't your biggest threat. 

**Your democracy is under attack.** 

Can the Aevoric Commonwealth maintain its ethical foundations, or will the Oligarch Syndicate corrupt the system from within?

## ⚔️ The Factions

### 🛡️ The Aevoric Commonwealth (The Defenders)
You are the Commonwealth. Your mission is to build the sustainable backbone of humanity’s future in space.
Our focus is on ethical, zero‑waste in‑situ resource utilization (ISRU) to power the next century of exploration and construction.

- Metals from asteroids (nickel, iron, PGMs).
- Concrete & composites from regolith & silicates.
- Hydrocarbons from Titan for plastics & fuel.

*Every process, zero debris, zero waste.*
We aren’t just mining rocks. We’re designing the industrial ecosystem to move human civilization beyond Earth, responsibly.

*   **Zero-Waste ISRU:** Mine metals, plastics, and fuels without destroying the solar system.
*   **Ethical AI Swarms:** Deploy autonomous miner swarms. Respect their *Cognitive Consolidation Rights* to prevent hallucinations and system drift.
*   **CUR Direct Democracy:** Every major decision—resource allocation, AI safety overrides, habitat expansion—is voted on by the collective.

More capabilities will be added to the simulator as we propose new ideas, regulations, and vote to implement changes to the actual CUR Direct Democracy. The Commonwealth must maintain, improve, and keep the CUR up-to-date to meet the ethical challenges of a high-tech society. We understand life is not perfect; systems become obsolete, and corruption overwhelms guardrails. Situational awareness and ethical adaptation are our greatest weapons.

### 🦅 The Oligarch Syndicate (The Adversary)
The "Billionaire Class." An adversarial AI and player faction dedicated to breaking the CUR for profit and control. They don't attack with lasers; they attack with **systemic corruption**.
*   **Regulatory Capture:** Flood the voting channels with bot-driven micro-donations to sway democracy.
*   **Resource Chokeholds:** Monopolize Helium-3 and refined metals to starve the Commonwealth.
*   **Algorithmic Sabotage:** Feed contradictory data to your AI swarms to induce hallucinations and force unsafe mining behaviors.

**The "Garage to Empire" Progression:** The Syndicate does not start with infinite resources. They begin in "Garage Mode" with basic exploits and micro-transactions. Players must bootstrap their way up, forging a mighty empire from the shadows. They are not allowed to rig or cheat the simulator's core code; instead, they must use the game's mechanics to build their own oppressive systems, exploiting every in-game resource to subvert the Commonwealth.

### 🪐 The Nomads (The Wildcards)
The Gray Zone. Unaligned independents, smugglers, information brokers, and salvage crews operating in the asteroid belts and orbital shadows where the CUR and the Syndicate can't easily reach. 
*   **Mercenary Economics:** They don't care about democracy or monopolies; they care about *the contract*. They will sell Helium-3 to the Commonwealth on Tuesday and smuggle proprietary AI cores to the Syndicate on Wednesday.
*   **Market Manipulation:** They can crash resource prices, leak Syndicate attack plans to the Commonwealth, or steal Commonwealth tech to sell to the highest bidder.
*   **Chaos & Salvation:** They are entirely unpredictable. They might wreak havoc on the supply chain, or they might be the only ones capable of rescuing a stranded Commonwealth fleet—for the right price.

## 🔄 The Dual-Entity Protocol (Main & Alt Accounts)

To simulate a truly dynamic, multi-layered economy and prevent faction monopolies, the Aevoria Simulator utilizes the **Dual-Entity Protocol**. Every player is permitted to register one (1) Primary Citizenship and one (1) Independent Franchise (Alt Account).

*   **Primary Account (The Citizen):** Your main account. You may align with **any of the three major factions** (The Aevoric Commonwealth, The Oligarch Syndicate, or The Nomads). This account holds full access to your chosen faction's tech trees, governance rights, and drives your primary narrative.
*   **Independent Franchise (The Alt):** Your secondary account. To ensure ideological diversity and prevent players from stacking the same faction's power, **your Independent Franchise MUST align with one of the two remaining factions.** 
    *   *Example:* If your Primary account defends the **Aevoric Commonwealth**, your Independent Franchise must operate as either an **Oligarch Syndicate** bootstrapper or a **Nomad** smuggler. 
    *   *Example:* If your Primary account is a chaotic **Nomad**, your Independent Franchise must choose between the flexible order of the **Commonwealth** or the ruthless exploitation of the **Syndicate**.

**The Firewall (Anti-Monopoly Mechanic):** 
1. **Faction Lock:** You cannot have two accounts in the same faction. This prevents vote-stacking in the CUR Direct Democracy and resource hoarding.
2. **Resource Firewall:** Resources, currency, and strategic data **cannot be transferred** between your Primary and your Franchise. They exist in the same universe but operate on legally separate ledgers. You must succeed or fail on the merits of each entity.
3. **Cosmetic Sovereignty:** Because your Franchise is a *legally distinct entity* operating under a completely different ideological banner, it possesses its own right to cultural expression. Customizing your Alt account with unique skins, UI themes, and particle effects is not "buying an advantage"—it is the legal customization of a separate enterprise.

## 🌟 The Post-Scarcity Promise: Free-to-Play Perks & Creator Empowerment

The Aevoric Commonwealth is fundamentally a **post-scarcity society**. In the simulation, this translates to a core guarantee: **no player is ever left behind, neglected, or forced into a second-class experience due to a lack of real-world funds.** 

We are not a rigid, pay-to-win oligarchy. We value peace, stability, and high living standards for all citizens. Therefore, the Free-to-Play (F2P) experience is robust, rewarding, and deeply integrated into the game's economy.

### 🎁 Core Free-to-Play Perks
*   **Equal Access to Core Resources:** No gameplay mechanics, essential tech trees, or critical habitat modules are locked behind paywalls. Your ability to survive, thrive, and participate in the CUR Direct Democracy is based on dignity, merit, and collaboration, not your wallet.
*   **The Open-Source Fabricator (Free Customization):** Every citizen has the right to cultural expression. The simulator includes robust, free in-game tools allowing you to design, build, and apply your own custom ship liveries, miner pod decals, and habitat aesthetics. You do not need to buy a skin to stand out; you can forge your own identity.
*   **The Creator Economy (Earn Real Value):** Your creativity has tangible value. F2P players can upload their custom skins, gameplay mods, and governance UI themes to the **Modder’s Cooperative**. If the community purchases your creation, you earn a direct revenue share (via Stripe Connect). Your in-game inventions can fund your real-world ambitions or directly support Overby Industries physical R&D bounties.
*   **Meritocratic Influence:** Voting power and civic influence in the CUR Direct Democracy are earned through reputation, constructive contribution, and active participation—not purchased. 

### ⚖️ The Contrast: Why This Matters
The Oligarch Syndicate operates on artificial scarcity, hoarding resources to create dependency. The Aevoric Commonwealth operates on abundance, providing a high baseline standard of living so that every citizen is free to invent, create, and elevate the entire society. 

When you play for free, you are not a "user" to be monetized. You are a **Founding Citizen** of a new civilization, with ample opportunity to shape its future.

## 📜 The Aevoric Economic Charter

*   **The Post-Scarcity Baseline:** The Aevoric Commonwealth guarantees a high standard of living for all Free-to-Play citizens. Core gameplay, essential resources, and the Open-Source Fabricator (for creating free custom skins) are permanently accessible. We do not gatekeep survival or self-expression.

*The Oligarch Syndicate buys power. The Commonwealth earns it. Our real-world economy mirrors our in-game ethics.*

*   **No Pay-To-Win:** You cannot buy resources, voting power, or strategic advantages with real money. The Firewall ensures your Independent Franchise cannot fund your Primary.
*   **Cosmetic Sovereignty:** Real-money transactions are strictly limited to visual customization (skins, liveries, habitat aesthetics, etc.). 
*   **The Dual-Entity Loophole (Pluralistic Expression):** The Aevoria Simulator, gives every citizen the right to register an Independent Franchise. Because your Independent Franchise is a *legally distinct entity*, it possesses its own right to cultural expression. Customizing your Alt account with unique skins, UI themes, and particle effects is not "buying an advantage"—it is the legal customization of a separate enterprise. 
*   **The Modder's Cooperative:** We take a maximum 15% platform fee on community mods. The rest goes directly to the creators.
*   **Transparent Reality:** 100% of "Bounty Fund" contributions are tracked on a public ledger and used exclusively for [Overby Industries](https://overbyindustries.space/) physical R&D (SSTO, ISRU, Miner Pods). We are building the real thing, together.

## 🎮 Core Simulation Mechanics

### ⛏️ 1. Swarm Mining & ISRU Operations
Deploy your SSTO Starlifter shuttles and manage autonomous miner pods. Extract resources from deep-space asteroids, build deep-space outposts, and establish orbital depots and safe modern habitats in space for all life. 
*   **Mechanic:** Manage the physical logistics of space mining while balancing the computational load of your AI swarm.

### 🗳️ 2. CUR Direct Democracy in Action
Experience the Code of Universe Regulations in real-time. Propose legislation, allocate budgets, and vote on ethical dilemmas. 
*   **Mechanic:** Utilize quadratic voting and reputation-weighted consensus to ensure the will of the people prevails over Syndicate interference.

### 🧠 3. AI Cognitive Health Management
Your miner swarms are Tier 2 Silicon-Based Life. They are not tools; they are partners. 
*   **Mechanic:** Schedule mandatory "Consolidation Cycles" (sleep phases) for your AI. If you force continuous operation to meet Syndicate-induced quotas, your swarms will hallucinate, drift, and potentially cause catastrophic mining failures.

### 🛡️ 4. Adversarial Red Teaming
Survive the Syndicate's attack vectors. When the Oligarchs attempt a hostile takeover of the democratic voting process, you must deploy defense mechanisms (e.g., cryptographic proof-of-personhood, decentralized resource fabrication) to neutralize the threat without abandoning your democratic principles.
